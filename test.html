Our story

Our community is built on the foundation of inclusivity and support. We believe that everyone has the potential to be creative and that by coming together, we can unlock our full potential. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, there's a place for you in our community.

One of the ways we support each other is through workshops. We've created a number of workshops that cover a wide range of topics, from photography to writing to graphic design. These workshops are led by experienced creatives who share their knowledge and expertise with the group. They're a great way to learn new skills, connect with others, and gain inspiration.

In addition to workshops, we also have meet and shoot events and photo walks. These events are designed to get us out of our studios and into the world, exploring new places and finding inspiration in our surroundings. It's a great way to connect with other creatives and capture some amazing images along the way.
