Education

Photography

The Photojournalism and Documentary Practice certificate program is a comprehensive, 5-month, full-time course designed to equip learners with the investigative skills and technical knowledge necessary for success in the dynamic field of visual storytelling. Led by accomplished instructors and renowned visiting photographers, this program provides a diverse range of skills and perspectives in news coverage, conceptual documentaries, editorial work, and multimedia projects. By enrolling, you will develop your own unique identity as a visual storyteller while gaining practical experience in the rapidly changing industry.

With a focus on integrity and authenticity, our program empowers learners to become influential storytellers with global impact. We prioritize learner-led initiatives, fostering a sense of community through class discussions and critiques.
